Duck and the beast

Once upon a time in a far away land there lift a beautiful duck with a white dress and the brightest smile of the whole kingdom, everyone loved to see her, except for one person. He wanted to grab her, put her in a dangerous dungeon so they could never see her back. He stalked her for weeks to plan everything and eventually put her in the dangerous dungeon. See tried to quack as hard as see could but nobody could hear her, she new there was no way out. But she wanted to know why she got locked in, the man was jealous of her and told her that she was so beautiful. The woman told her I can give you everything to make you also this beautiful, the man let her go and the lady pushed the man in a deep pit and she lived happily ever after.
