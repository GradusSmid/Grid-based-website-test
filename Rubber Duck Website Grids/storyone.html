Drunk duck

Once upon a time in a far away land was a duck that got very drunk. He drank Jägermeister and jodeld all day, he didn't need any friends and had fun by himself. There was only one thing he loved more than Jägermeister, it was Da Tweekaz worlds best hardstyle dj's, he went to every festival to see them play. everyday he traveled the world with a few bottles of Jägermeister to see them. Without any trouble he could see them and party with them. But don't stand in his way because he will kick your ass. This duck died 4 weeks later because of a alcohol poisoning.
